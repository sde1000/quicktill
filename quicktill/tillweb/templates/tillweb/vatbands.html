VAT bands are used by departments to set the business that receives payments from sales in the department, and the percentage of VAT that has been charged on those sales.

Both the business that receives the funds, and the percentage rate of VAT, can change over time. The current rate and business are shown below for each band; click through to the detail page to see the table of changes over time.

The "band" letter is used when printing receipts that have lines that cover more than one VAT band; the letter is printed at the end of each transaction line, and the corresponding business and rate information is printed at the end of the receipt.
